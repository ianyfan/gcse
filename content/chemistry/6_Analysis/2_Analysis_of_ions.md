1. ###Flame tests

    |Metal ions|Flame colour|
    |:--------:|:----------:|
    | Lithium  |  Crimson   |
    |  Sodium  |   Yellow   |
    |Potassium |   Lilac    |
    | Calcium  |    Red     |
    |  Barium  |   Green    |
2. ###Reaction with sodium hydroxide

    |     Metal ion      | Colour of precipitate formed |
    |:------------------:|:----------------------------:|
    |Calcium<br>Magnesium|            White             |
    |     Aluminium      |White, but dissolves in excess|
    |    Copper (II)     |             Blue             |
    |     Iron (II)      |            Green             |
    |     Iron (III)     |            Brown             |

        | Halide |Colour|
